Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Wordrops (hereinafter “we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects the personal data of users participating in our platform through the website wordrops.com.

Data Collected

  • Name: to identify you.
  • Email: to send you notifications regarding platform usage and updates.
  • Preferences: to better understand your expectations and improve your experience.
  • Platform interactions: to analyze platform usage, including stories created and shared.
  • Browsing data: collected via Google Analytics (through Google Tag Manager), including pages visited, visit duration, and traffic source.
  • Cookies: to collect browsing data and improve user experience

How we use your data

  • Provide and improve our platform and services.
  • Analyze user interactions to optimize platform experience and functionality.
  • Send you email notifications for updates, new features, and other relevant communications.
  • Improve platform content based on user feedback and interactions.

Data Retention

Personal data collected is stored in our secure database. We use secure hosting platforms but do not share your data with third parties unless necessary for service operation or to comply with legal obligations.

7. External Service Providers and Data Processing

Railway implements appropriate security measures to protect stored data, in compliance with international privacy and data protection regulations.

Data Protection

We adopt adequate security measures to protect your personal data, including encryption technologies and other security practices to prevent unauthorized access.
