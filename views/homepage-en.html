HOW DOES IT WORK?

1

Join the story

Jump in with 4 other unknown writers and start creating a unique story. Each time, the plot unfolds unpredictably — this is the perfect chance to challenge your creativity! Get ready to be surprised with every new chapter.

2

Write your chapter

When it’s your turn, you’ll have a set time to add your personal touch to the story. But beware: each chapter must weave seamlessly with the previous ones. Build on others’ creativity and push the plot forward with your unique style. Every word counts to keep the flow alive!

3

The story is complete

After 5 rounds, your team will have crafted an epic story (hopefully). Depending on the mode you chose, the game plays out differently.


Ranked: Rate others’ chapters and get rated yourself. Every impactful contribution earns you points to climb the global leaderboard.


Classic: No votes, no scores. Just the joy of writing and having fun together, no pressure.

4

Challenge your creativity

Every completed story and chapter you write helps you climb the leaderboard and prove to everyone who the best storyteller is. On this platform, collaboration and competition always go hand in hand!
